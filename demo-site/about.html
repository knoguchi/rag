About Demo Cloud

Building the infrastructure that powers the next generation of applications.

Our Mission

At Demo Cloud, we believe that world-class infrastructure should be accessible to everyone. Whether you're a solo developer building your first app or an enterprise serving millions of users, you deserve reliable, secure, and affordable cloud services.

We founded Demo Cloud in 2020 with a simple goal: make cloud computing simple. No more wrestling with complex configurations, unpredictable bills, or confusing interfaces. Just powerful infrastructure that works.

Our Values

Simplicity

We believe powerful tools don't have to be complicated. Every feature we build is designed to be intuitive and easy to use.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprise bills, no confusing pricing. You always know exactly what you're paying for.

Our Team

We're a team of engineers, designers, and cloud enthusiasts who've built and scaled infrastructure at companies like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft. We've experienced the pain of managing cloud infrastructure firsthand, and we're building the solution we wished existed.

Demo Cloud is headquartered in San Francisco, with team members across North America, Europe, and Asia. We're a remote-first company that values work-life balance and continuous learning.
