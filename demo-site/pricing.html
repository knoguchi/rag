Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new accounts receive $100 in free credits that never expire. This is enough to run a small application for several months.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll be prorated for any differences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and ACH bank transfers. Enterprise customers can pay via wire transfer or purchase order.

Is there a commitment?

No commitments required. Pay-as-you-go pricing means you can scale up or down anytime. Reserved instances are available for those who want additional discounts.

What happens if I exceed plan limits?

You'll be billed at pay-as-you-go rates for any usage beyond your plan's included resources. We'll notify you before you exceed limits.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for qualified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact us to apply.
